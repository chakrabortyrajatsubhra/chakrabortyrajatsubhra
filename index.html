More About Me

I work at the intersection of interpretability, fairness, and bias in generative AI — currently focused on discovering and mitigating social biases in Text-to-Image diffusion models. Earlier work spans multimodal understanding of large vision-language models and semi-supervised object detection. My undergraduate thesis tackled character recognition in Devanagari and Bengali script.
